LONG DESCRIPTION (≤ 10,000 characters)
----------------------------------------
Take full control of your finances with Bank Statement Analyzer — the intelligent XLSX App that transforms raw bank data into clear, actionable insights in seconds.

Whether you're a freelancer tracking business expenses, a family managing a household budget, or a financial professional preparing client reports, Bank Statement Analyzer does the heavy lifting for you.

──────────────────────────────────────
HOW IT WORKS
──────────────────────────────────────
1. Upload your bank statement — supports CSV, XLSX, and PDF formats from virtually any bank or financial institution.
2. The app automatically maps columns, parses transactions, and groups them into 14 standard spending categories (Groceries, Utilities, Transport, Entertainment, Healthcare, Dining, Shopping, Travel, Transfers, Subscriptions, Rent/Mortgage, Insurance, Savings, and Other).
3. Review interactive charts, a financial health score, top merchant breakdowns, and monthly trend analysis — all on one screen.
4. Export a professional PDF report or share a summary via email, ready to hand off to an accountant or advisor.

──────────────────────────────────────
KEY FEATURES
──────────────────────────────────────
★ Multi-format Import
   Upload CSV, XLSX (.xlsx / .xls), or PDF bank statements. Paste raw CSV text directly if you prefer — no file needed.

★ Smart Auto-Categorization
   14-category intelligent transaction classifier automatically sorts your spending. Manually override any category with a single tap.

★ Financial Health Score
   Receive a personalized 0–100 score based on your income-to-expense ratio, savings rate, spending diversity, and transaction volatility.

★ Rich Visualizations
   • Monthly income vs. expenses bar chart
   • Category-level donut chart with percentage breakdowns
   • Top 5 merchants ranked by spend
   • Day-of-week spending heatmap
   • Running balance trend line

★ Advanced Search & Filter
   Search transactions by merchant, amount, date range, or category. Filter results instantly with no page reload.

★ Professional PDF Reports
   One-tap PDF export with your logo, a full transaction table, category summary, and key KPIs — perfect for accountants, auditors, or personal records.

★ Email Delivery
   Send a polished HTML financial report directly to any email address from within the app.

★ Multi-Currency Support
   Switch between 10+ currencies (USD, EUR, GBP, NGN, KES, GHS, ZAR, INR, CAD, AUD). All amounts display in your chosen currency symbol.

★ Pro Subscription (USDT Crypto Payments)
   Unlock unlimited statement uploads, advanced charts, email delivery, and PDF export with a Pro license. Pay securely via USDT on Tron, Binance Smart Chain, or Ethereum — no bank card required.

★ Privacy-First Design
   All processing happens locally in your browser. No transaction data is ever stored on our servers. Your financial information stays completely private.

★ Works Offline (PWA)
   Install as a Progressive Web App on Windows, Android, iOS, or any modern browser. Use core features without an internet connection once installed.

★ Accessible & Responsive
   Fully responsive layout adapts to desktop, tablet, and mobile screens. Keyboard accessible and screen-reader friendly.
